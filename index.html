What is Giving Garden?

Or we should say, what will Giving Garden be?
We haven't launched yet, but we have big plans to build an app to help you:

Grow

Don’t know what to grow in your area? Need reminders about watering or harvesting? We’ll have you covered with realtime, crowdsourced and local data so you can grow the best food with the help of data and tech at your fingertips.

Give and Get

Got too many lemon cucumbers, but no lemons? Put your extra produce up on the GivingGarden marketplace and find what you need from your local community of GivingGardeners. It’s a win win win. That third win is for our planet. Less waste. Check.

Connect

Ever notice that the best gardening advice is usually from someone in your neighborhood. Well, don’t wait to bump into those human gardening books. Connect with them, local farmers, Master Gardeners and Preservers, and other healthy food-loving folks like you.
